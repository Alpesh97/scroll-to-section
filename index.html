About

Chokhani Projects is a sub-venture of Chokhani Group - a diversified group of companies that isn’t restricted to construction but has extended its reach into multiple sectors of businesses over the years. Chokhani Projects aims at becoming the key construction partners for the Pharmaceutical, Chemical, and Food industries. Chokhani Constructions, Yash Constructions, and Chokhani Projects are the three companies by which Chokhani group is into the business of leading industrial construction. All three of the companies have been serving clients successfully with honesty and dedication for more than 3 decades now and are optimistic for even higher achievements, setting new milestones along the journey towards excellence.
